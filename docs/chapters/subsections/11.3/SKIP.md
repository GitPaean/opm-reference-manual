### SKIP -- Activate Skipping of All Keywords and Input Data

The [SKIP](#__RefHeading___Toc52489_2479612490) keyword activates skipping of all keywords and input data until the [ENDSKIP](#__RefHeading___Toc52491_2479612490) keyword is encountered. All keywords between the [SKIP](#__RefHeading___Toc52489_2479612490) and [ENDSKIP](#__RefHeading___Toc52491_2479612490) keywords are ignored.
