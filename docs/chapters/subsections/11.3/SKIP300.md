### SKIP300 -- Activate Skipping of Compositional Keywords and Input Data

Turns on skipping of "compositional" keywords until the [ENDSKIP](#__RefHeading___Toc52491_2479612490) activation keyword is encountered. All "compositional" keywords between the [SKIP300](#__RefHeading___Toc980121_1781444514) and [ENDSKIP](#__RefHeading___Toc52491_2479612490) keywords are ignored by OPM Flow.
