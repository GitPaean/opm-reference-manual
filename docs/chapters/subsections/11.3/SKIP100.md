### SKIP100 -- Activate Skipping of Black-Oil Keywords and Input Data

This keyword turns on skipping of black-oil keywords until the ENDSKIP activation keyword is encountered. All black-oil keywords between the SKIP100 and ENDSKIP keywords are ignored by OPM Flow.

See [SKIP100 -- Activate Skipping of Black-Oil Keywords and Input Data](#anchor-1) in the GLOBAL section for a full description.
