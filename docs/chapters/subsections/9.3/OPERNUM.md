### OPERNUM -- Define Regions for Mathematical Operations on Arrays

This keyword defines the [OPERATE](#__RefHeading___Toc64455_718313858) region numbers for each grid block. The [OPERATE](#__RefHeading___Toc64455_718313858) keyword defines mathematical operations on arrays in the commercial simulator and is currently not supported by OPM Flow. However, similar functionality is provided by the [ADD](#__RefHeading___Toc4412_421927891) and [MULTIPLY](#__RefHeading___Toc296609_1576177388) keywords combined with [MULTNUM](#__RefHeading___Toc61329_2752266063) region array.

See [[OPERNUM -- Define Regions for Mathematical Operations on Arrays](#anchor-1)](#5.3.60.OPERNUM – Define Regions for Mathematical Operations on Arrays|outline) in the [GRID](#__RefHeading___Toc38674_784232322) section for a full description.
