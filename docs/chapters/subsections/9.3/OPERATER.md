### OPERATER -- Define Mathematical Operations on Arrays by Region

This keyword defines the mathematical operations on arrays for specific regions in the commercial simulator and is currently not supported by OPM Flow. However, similar functionality is provided by the [ADD](#__RefHeading___Toc4412_421927891) and [MULTIPLY](#__RefHeading___Toc296609_1576177388) keywords.

See [[OPERATER -- Define Mathematical Operations on Arrays by Region](#anchor-1)](#6.3.65.OPERATER – Define Mathematical Operations on Arrays by Region|outline) in the [GRID](#__RefHeading___Toc38674_784232322) section for a full description.
