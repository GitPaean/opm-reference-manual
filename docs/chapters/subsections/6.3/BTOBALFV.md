### BTOBALFV -- Dual Porosity Matrix to Fracture Multiplier (Individual Cells)

| RUNSPEC | GRID | EDIT | PROPS | REGIONS | SOLUTION | SUMMARY | SCHEDULE |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

#### Description

The BTOBALFV keyword defines a dual porosity matrix to fracture multiplier that is applied to individual cells, for when the Dual Porosity model has been invoked by either the DUALPORO or the DUALPERM keywords in the RUNSPEC section.

See also the BTOBALFAV keyword in the GRID section that applies a constant multiplier to all cells.

This keyword is not supported by OPM Flow but would change the results if supported so the simulation will be stopped.
