### WPOTCALC -- Well Potential Calculation Options

| RUNSPEC | GRID | EDIT | PROPS | REGIONS | SOLUTION | SUMMARY | SCHEDULE |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

#### Description

WPOTCALC defines how shut-in and stopped wells should have their well potentials calculated. Well potentials for wells under these conditions need to have their potentials calculated if they are in a Priority Drilling Queue via the WDRILPRI keyword in the SCHEDULE section, or the Prioritization option has been enabled by the PRIORITY keyword in the SCHEDULE section.

This keyword is not supported by OPM Flow but has no effect on the results so it will be ignored.
