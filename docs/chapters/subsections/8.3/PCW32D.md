### PCW32D -- Water-Oil Capillary Pressure versus Oil and Gas Saturation Tables

| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

#### Description

This keyword, PCW32D, enables the water-oil capillary pressure data to be entered as a function of both oil and gas saturations. The keyword should be used in conjunction with the SWF32D keyword in the PROPS section. See also the PCG32D keyword in the PROPS section that provides similar functionality for the gas-oil capillary pressure data.

This keyword is not supported by OPM Flow but would change the results if supported so the simulation will be stopped.
