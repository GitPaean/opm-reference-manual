### FOAMMOBP -- Define Foam Mobility Reduction versus Oil Pressure

| RUNSPEC | GRID | EDIT | PROPS | REGIONS | SOLUTION | SUMMARY | SCHEDULE |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

#### Description

The FOAMMOBP keyword defines the reduction in foam mobility reduction versus oil pressure, for when the Foam option has been activated by the FOAM keyword in the RUNSPEC.

This keyword is not supported by OPM Flow but would change the results if supported so the simulation will be stopped.
