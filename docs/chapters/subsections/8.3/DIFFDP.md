### DIFFDP -- Activate Dual Porosity Molecular Diffusion for Matrix-Fracture Flow Only

| RUNSPEC | GRID | EDIT | PROPS | REGIONS | SOLUTION | SUMMARY | SCHEDULE |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

#### Description

This keyword, DIFFDP, activates the dual porosity molecular diffusion for matrix-fracture flow only option for when the Dual Porosity option has be activated by either the DUALPORO or DUALPERM keywords, and the Diffusivity option has been activated by the DIFFUSE keywords; three keywords are in the RUNSPEC section.

This keyword is not supported by OPM Flow but would change the results if supported so the simulation will be stopped.
