#### Description

The PEKTAB series of keywords define a series of coefficients of a polynomial function used in the calculation of the bulk modulus in the petro-elastic model. The series of keywords consist of: PEKTAB0 PEKTAB1, PEKTAB2, PEKTAB3, PEKTAB4, PEKTAB5, PEKTAB6, and PEKTAB7.

This series of keywords are ignored by OPM Flow and have no effect on the simulation.
