### RKTRMDIR -- Activate ROCKTAB Keyword Directional Transmissibility Multipliers

| RUNSPEC | GRID | EDIT | PROPS | REGIONS | SOLUTION | SUMMARY | SCHEDULE |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

#### Description

This keyword activates the directional transmissibility multipliers for the ROCKTAB keyword. This results in two additional columns being inputted on the ROCKTAB keyword. This feature is currently not supported in OPM Flow.

This keyword is not supported by OPM Flow but would change the results if supported so the simulation will be stopped.
