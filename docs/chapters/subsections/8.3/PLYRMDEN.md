### PLYRMDEN -- Define Polymer Model In Situ Rock Density

| RUNSPEC | GRID | EDIT | PROPS | REGIONS | SOLUTION | SUMMARY | SCHEDULE |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

#### Description

The PLYRMDEN keyword defines the in situ rock density at reservoir conditions by individual cell, for when the Polymer option has been activated by the POLYMER keyword in the RUNSPEC section. PLYRMDEN should consist of an array of real positive values. See the DENSITY parameter on the PLYROCK keyword in the PROPS section for setting the property for the whole grid.
