### TEMPTVD -- Activate Temperature Flux Limited Transport Option

| RUNSPEC | GRID | EDIT | PROPS | REGIONS | SOLUTION | SUMMARY | SCHEDULE |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

#### Description

TEMPTVD activates the Temperature Flux Limited Transport option in the commercial simulator, to reduce numerical dispersion for when either the TEMP or THERMAL keywords in the RUNSPEC section have been declared.

This keyword is not supported by OPM Flow but would change the results if supported so the simulation will be stopped.
