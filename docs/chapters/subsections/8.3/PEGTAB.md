### PEGTAB -- Petro-Elastic Pressure Shear Modulus Table

#### Description

The PEGTAB series of keywords define a series of coefficients of a polynomial function used in the calculation of the shear modulus in the petro-elastic model. The series of keywords consist of: PEGTAB0 PEGTAB1, PEGTAB2, PEGTAB3, PEGTAB4, PEGTAB5, PEGTAB6, and PEGTAB7.

This series of keywords are ignored by OPM Flow and have no effect on the simulation.
