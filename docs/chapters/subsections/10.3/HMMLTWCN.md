### HMMLTWCN -- History Match Well Connection and Skin Multipliers

| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

#### Description

This keyword, HMMLTWCN, defines the history match gradient multipliers for well connection factors and connection skins, for when the History Match Gradient option has been activated by the HMDIMS keyword in the RUNSPEC section. Wells must be specified using the WELPSECS keyword in the SCHEDULE section and their connections defined by the COMPDAT and/or COMPDATL keywords, also in the SCHEDULE section

This keyword is not supported by OPM Flow but would change the results if supported so the simulation will be stopped.
