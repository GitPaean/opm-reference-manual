### MULTR- -- Multiply Cell Transmissibility in the -R Direction

[MULTX-](#__RefHeading___Toc80285_1778172979) multiples the transmissibility between two cell faces in the -X direction for all the cells in the model via an array, that is the keyword sets the transmissibility multiplier of block (I, J, K) between the cells (I-1, J, K) and (I, J, K). The keyword should only be used with radial grids, as declared by the [RADIAL](#__RefHeading___Toc51752_2905512151) keyword in the [RUNSPEC](#__RefHeading___Toc55591_1778172979) section.

See [[MULTR- - Multiply Cell Transmissibility in the -R Direction](#anchor-1)](#6.3.116.MULTR- - Multiply Cell Transmissibility in the -R Direction|outline) in the [GRID](#__RefHeading___Toc38674_784232322) section for a full description.
