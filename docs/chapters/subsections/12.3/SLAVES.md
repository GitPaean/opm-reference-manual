### SLAVES -- Define Slave Reservoir Simulation Parameters

| RUNSPEC | GRID | EDIT | PROPS | REGIONS | SOLUTION | SUMMARY | SCHEDULE |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

#### Description

This keyword, SLAVES, defines the name of the slave reservoirs and their associated simulation input files, for when the Reservoir Coupling option has been declared active by the GRUPMAST and SLAVES keywords in the SCHEDULE section.

This keyword is not supported by OPM Flow but would change the results if supported so the simulation will be stopped.
