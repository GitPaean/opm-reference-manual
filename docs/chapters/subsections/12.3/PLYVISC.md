### PLYVISC -- Define Polymer Viscosity Scaling Fact

PLYSVISC defines the polymer viscosity scaling factors used to determine the relationship of pure water viscosity with respect to increasing polymer saturation within a grid block. The polymer option must be activated by the [POLYMER](#__RefHeading___Toc38609_2267116897) keyword in the [RUNSPEC](#__RefHeading___Toc55591_1778172979) section in order to use this keyword.

See [[PLYVISC -- Define Polymer Viscosity Scaling Factors](#anchor-1)](#7.2.41.PLYVISC – Define Polymer Viscosity Scaling Factors|outline) in the [PROPS](#__RefHeading___Toc39329_784232322) section for a full description.
