### MULTX -- Multiply Cell Transmissibility in the +X Direction

[MULTX](#__RefHeading___Toc80283_1778172979) multiples the transmissibility between two cell faces in the +X direction for all the cells in the model via an array, that is the keyword sets the transmissibility multiplier of block (I, J, K) between the cells (I, J, K) and (I+I, J, K).

See [[MULTX - Multiply Cell Transmissibility in the +X Direction](#anchor-1)](#5.3.39.MULTX - Multiply Cell Transmissibility in the +X Direction|outline) in the [GRID](#__RefHeading___Toc38674_784232322) section for a full description.
