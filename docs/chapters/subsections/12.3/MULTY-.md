### MULTY- -- Multiply Cell Transmissibility in the -Y Direction

MULTY- multiples the transmissibility between two cell faces in the -Y direction for all the cells in the model via an array, that is the keyword sets the transmissibility multiplier of block (I, J, K) between the cells (I, J-1, K) and (I, J, K).

See [MULTY- - Multiply Cell Transmissibility in the -Y Direction](#anchor-1) in the GRID section for a full description.
