### WELDRAW -- Define Maximum Draw Down for Production Wells

| RUNSPEC | GRID | EDIT | PROPS | REGIONS | SOLUTION | SUMMARY | SCHEDULE |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

#### Description

This keyword, WELDRAW, defines the maximum draw down for production wells The keyword may be useful in wells that are subject to fines or sand production to limit the draw down between the sand face and the well in order to limit or avoid sand production.

This keyword is not supported by OPM Flow but would change the results if supported so the simulation will be stopped.
