### CECONT -- Define Well Connections Tracer Economic Limit Criteria

| RUNSPEC | GRID | EDIT | PROPS | REGIONS | SOLUTION | SUMMARY | SCHEDULE |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

#### Description

This keyword, CECONT, sets the tracer economic cut-off criteria for a well's connection to the simulation grid.

This keyword is not supported by OPM Flow but would change the results if supported so the simulation will be stopped.
