### MULTX- -- Multiply Cell Transmissibility in the -X Direction

[MULTX-](#__RefHeading___Toc80285_1778172979) multiples the transmissibility between two cell faces in the -X direction for all the cells in the model via an array, that is the keyword sets the transmissibility multiplier of block (I, J, K) between the cells (I-1, J, K) and (I, J, K).

See [[MULTX- - Multiply Cell Transmissibility in the -X Direction](#anchor-1)](#5.3.40.MULTX- - Multiply Cell Transmissibility in the -X Direction|outline) in the [GRID](#__RefHeading___Toc38674_784232322) section for a full description.
