### PLYSHEAR -- Activate and Define Polymer Shearing Parameters

The [PLYSHEAR](#__RefHeading___Toc110218_2939291539) keyword activates and the defines the polymer shear thinning-thickening option for when the polymer option has been activated by the [POLYMER](#__RefHeading___Toc38609_2267116897) keyword in the [RUNSPEC](#__RefHeading___Toc55591_1778172979) section.
