### MULTTHT -- Multiply Cell Transmissibility in the +Theta Direction

[MULTTHT](#__RefHeading___Toc270099_1841740821) multiples the transmissibility between two cell faces in the +Theta direction for all the cells in the model via an array, that is the keyword sets the transmissibility multiplier of block (I, J, K) between the cells (I, J, K) and (I, J+1, K). The keyword should only be used with radial grids, as declared by the [RADIAL](#__RefHeading___Toc51752_2905512151) keyword in the [RUNSPEC](#__RefHeading___Toc55591_1778172979) section.

See [[MULTTHT - Multiply Cell Transmissibility in the +Theta Direction](#anchor-1)](#6.3.121.MULTTHT - Multiply Cell Transmissibility in the +Theta Direction|outline) in the [GRID](#__RefHeading___Toc38674_784232322) section for a full description.
