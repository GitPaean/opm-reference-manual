### MULTX- -- Multiply Cell Transmissibility in the -X Direction

MULTX- multiples the transmissibility between two cell faces in the -X direction for all the cells in the model via an array, that is the keyword sets the transmissibility multiplier of block (I, J, K) between the cells (I-1, J, K) and (I, J, K).

See [MULTX- - Multiply Cell Transmissibility in the -X Direction](#anchor-1) in the GRID section for a full description.
